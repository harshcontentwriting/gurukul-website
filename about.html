Bhaktivedanta Vidyabhavan Gurukula

Nurturing souls in the sacred tradition of Vedic education and the Daiva Varṇāśrama Dharma.

Our Foundational Mission

The **Bhaktivedanta Vidyabhavan Gurukula (BVG)** was established under the direct inspiration and guidance of **H.H. Bhakti Raghava Swami Maharaj** to serve as a practical example of the Daiva Varṇāśrama system in modern India.

Our primary goal is to produce well-rounded individuals—**Devotees with character, competence, and compassion**—who are equally adept in **spiritual knowledge (Shiksha)**, **modern academics (NIOS)**, and **practical skills (Seva)**.

  • **Go-rakshya:** Protection and care of cows as central to the community.
  • **Holistic Education:** Blending Vedic wisdom with essential modern curriculum.
  • **Self-Sufficiency:** Training in farming, cooking, and maintenance skills.
