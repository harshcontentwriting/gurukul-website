Frequently Asked Questions

We primarily accept students starting from the age of **5 years (Kindergarten)** through **Standard 5**. Admissions into higher classes are considered on a case-by-case basis depending on the student's background and our available capacity.

Our curriculum allocates dedicated time for both. The mornings focus on **Sadhana** (spiritual practice) and **Vedic Studies** (Sanskrit, scripture). The late mornings are reserved for **Academic Studies** following the **MSBOS/NIOS** curriculum, ensuring all students receive recognized modern education. See the full Academics page for details.

The Gurukula philosophy is based on **Seva** (selfless service). We do not charge mandatory tuition fees. However, we do request **voluntary contributions** to help cover the costs of daily operations, food (prasadam), and student supplies. Specific contribution guidelines are provided during the admission discussion.

  1. **Submit Inquiry:** Fill out the form on this page.
  2. **Initial Contact:** Our team will call you to discuss the Gurukula's expectations and your child's needs.
  3. **Interview:** A formal online or in-person interview with the parents and the child is scheduled.
  4. **Formal Acceptance:** Upon acceptance, documentation is completed, and we welcome the child to the Gurukula.
