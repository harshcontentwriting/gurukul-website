Our Upcoming Projects

Your support helps us build a brighter future for our students. Explore our current needs and join us in our seva.

1. Gaushala Development

A well-equipped shelter to provide our cows with a safe, comfortable, and all-weather home, essential for Gau Seva.

Key Requirements:

  • Exhaust and ceiling fans for proper ventilation.
  • Warm lighting and comfortable cow mats.
  • Dedicated water tank and fodder storage.
Sponsor this Seva

2. Empowering Our Teachers

Providing humble, dignified living spaces for our dedicated teachers, empowering them to focus fully on Shiksha, Sadhana, and Seva.

A Humble Space for Seva:

  • Attached toilet and bathroom.
  • Basic furniture (almira, bed, table, chair).
  • Essential amenities (fan, light, Wi-Fi connection).
Support Our Teachers

3. Yagyashala Construction

A sacred space essential for our students' *sadhana*, allowing them to learn and perform Vedic yagya ceremonies.

Needs for Sacred Space:

  • Construction of a dedicated Yagyashala structure.
  • Procurement of yagya equipment and *patras*.
  • Stocking of yagya *paraphernalia* for future ceremonies.
Contribute to Sadhana

4. Guest Rooms

Providing simple, clean, and humble accommodation for visiting devotees, parents, and scholars.

Room Facilities:

  • Simple rooms with attached toilet and bathroom.
  • Basic furniture (table and chair).
  • Simple bed for Matajis, *chatai* (mats) for Prabhujis.
Help Welcome Guests

5. Temple Hall

Constructing a dedicated and inspiring space for our beloved Deities, daily worship, and large devotional gatherings.

Key Requirements:

  • Construction of a dedicated space for the Deities.
  • Required *Arati* paraphernalia (bells, lamps, etc.).
  • High-quality, easy-to-clean marble or tiled flooring.
Sponsor the Temple

6. Setting up Vegetable Farms

Developing our surrounding land to grow our own organic vegetables and grains, promoting a self-sufficient community (Go-rakshya & Krishi).

Needs for Organic Farming:

  • Organic seeds and necessary gardening tools.
  • Installation of an efficient water irrigation system.
  • Secure fencing to protect crops from wild animals.
Support Self-Sufficiency

7. Library for Students

Creating a dedicated and peaceful space for students to study, research, and gain access to essential spiritual and academic texts.

Key Requirements:

  • New shelving units, tables, and seating.
  • Procurement of diverse book collection (Vedic/Academic).
  • Basic digital tools for student research.
Sponsor a Book

8. Varnashrama Community

The long-term vision of developing a sustainable, spiritually centered community based on Vedic principles (Go, Krishi, and Grāma).

Development Needs:

  • Land planning and preparation/zoning.
  • Construction of simple, eco-friendly housing models.
  • Community borewell and water storage solution.
Support the Vision
