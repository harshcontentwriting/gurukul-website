Our Upcoming Projects

Your support helps us build a brighter future for our students. Explore our current needs and join us in our seva.

1. Gaushala Development

Our cows are here, but we face challenges during the rainy season. Our goal is to build a well-equipped Gaushala to provide them with a safe and comfortable home.

Key Requirements:

  • Exhaust fans and ceiling fans for proper ventilation.
  • Warm lighting for the colder months.
  • Comfortable cow mats and an ample supply of fodder.
  • A dedicated water tank for clean drinking water.
Sponsor this Seva

2. Empowering Our Teachers

Our teachers are the heart of the Gurukula, dedicating their lives to Shiksha, Sadhna, and Seva. To ensure they can serve our students with full focus and peace of mind, it is our duty to provide them with a humble and supportive living space. This project is essential for the betterment and stability of our Gurukul.

A Humble Space for Seva:

  • Attached toilet and bathroom.
  • Basic furniture: an almira, bed, table, and chair.
  • Essential amenities: fan, light, and Wi-Fi connection.
  • A hot water kettle for convenience.
Support Our Teachers

3. Yagyashala Construction

A Yagyashala is essential for our students' sadhana and is a vital part of their spiritual shiksha. We aim to build one so our students can learn and perform these sacred ceremonies.

Needs:

  • Construction of a dedicated Yagyashala structure.
  • Procurement of yagya equipment and patras.
  • Stocking of yagya paraphernalia for future ceremonies.
Contribute to Sadhana

4. Guest Rooms

We wish to provide simple, clean, and humble accommodation for visiting devotees and guests, ensuring a comfortable stay.

Room Facilities:

  • Simple rooms with attached toilet and bathroom.
  • Basic furniture: one table and one chair.
  • For Matajis' rooms, a simple bed will be provided.
  • For Prabhujis' rooms, chatai or mats will be provided.
