Our Vision: Daiva Varṇāśrama Dharma

Established under the guidance of **H.H. Bhakti Raghava Swami Maharaj**, BVG Murbad is dedicated to practically reviving the principles of **Daiva Varṇāśrama Dharma**—a God-centered, agrarian, self-sufficient community life.

Our Gurukula is more than a school; it is a laboratory for character development, teaching students their eternal relationship with the Supreme Lord and their duty to society through service, simplicity, and compassion.
